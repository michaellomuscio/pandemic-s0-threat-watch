How this works

The Threat Deck is the deck you draw from each turn to place surveillance on cities. When a city's incident track fills, an Incident triggers — and the entire discard pile is shuffled and placed back on top of the deck.

That means the cards you've already seen since the last Incident are about to come up again. You don't know the order — but you know the set. That's intel you can plan around.

  1. Tap a city when it's drawn → moves to Discard.
  2. Press INCIDENT when one triggers → Discard becomes the "On Top" pile.
  3. Tap "On Top" cards as they come up — these are the only cards that can come out until the pile is empty.
  4. Long-press a card to remove it from the deck (legacy events sometimes do this).

Multiple Incidents stack: cards from the most recent reshuffle come up before older "on top" cards. The badge on each card shows which Incident it came from.
