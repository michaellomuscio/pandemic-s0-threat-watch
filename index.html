How to use this
  1. Tap a city when it's drawn from the Threat Deck.
  2. Press ⚠ INCIDENT when one triggers in-game. The discard pile moves to "On Top of Deck."
  3. Tap On-Top cards as they come up — you now know the set of cities coming next.
  4. Long-press any card for more options (surveillance, safehouse, remove from deck).

Want the campaign HUD (month, threat level), surveillance auto-tracking, or filters? Use the toggles up top — they're hidden by default to keep things simple.
